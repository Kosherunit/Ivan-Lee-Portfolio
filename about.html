About the Creator

Ivan Lee

Visionary Filmmaker & Cinematographer

With over a decade of experience in the film industry, Ivan Lee has established himself as a leading cinematographer and filmmaker in Southeast Asia. His work spans across commercial campaigns, corporate films, and artistic projects that have garnered international recognition.

Based in Malaysia, Ivan combines technical expertise with creative vision to deliver compelling visual narratives that resonate with audiences worldwide. His collaborative approach with brands like DJI, GUCCI, and major tourism boards has resulted in award-winning campaigns that push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Ivan's philosophy centers on authenticity and emotional connection, believing that every frame should serve the story and every story should touch the human experience. This approach has made him a sought-after partner for brands and organizations looking to create meaningful visual content.
