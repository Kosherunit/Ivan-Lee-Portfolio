About the Creator

Ivan Lee

Photographer/Filmmaker and your occasional Web Developer.

With over a decade of experience in the film industry, Ivan Lee has established himself as a leading photographers and filmmaker in Southeast Asia and some parts of Europe and Australia. His work spans across commercial campaigns, corporate films, and artistic projects that have garnered international and local recognition.

Based in Penang Island, Malaysia. Ivan combines technical expertise with creative vision to deliver compelling visual narratives that resonate with audiences worldwide. His collaborative approach with brands like DJI, Air Asia, and major tourism boards such as Western Australia Tourism has resulted in award-winning campaigns that push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Ivan's philosophy centers on authenticity and emotional connection, believing that every frame should serve the story and every story should touch the human experience. This approach has made him a sought-after partner for brands and organizations looking to create meaningful visual content.
